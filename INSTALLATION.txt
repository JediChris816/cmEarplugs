Copy the files in the mission folder to your mission folder.

	or if you already have some of them in your mission folder..
	copy over the cmEARPLUGS code blocks in each file to yours.

Edit the follwoing in your battleye filters.

at the end of the first line in publicvariable.txt add this:

	!="HasEarplugMenu"


at the end of the first line in setvariables.txt add this:


	!="HasEarplugMenu"



at the end of your removeAllActions line in your scripts.txt add this

	!="onPlayerKilled.sqf"

at the end of your execvm line in your scripts.txt add this


	!="[] execVM "cmEarplugs\cm_veh_fun.sqf";"
